Sesame beef with gochujang udon noodles

You will require: To prepare follow the instrution:
400g skirt beef steak Place the skirt steak on a large oven tray, season with salt flakes and drizzle with the sesame oil. Leave steak to stand at room temperature for 30 minutes or until the fridge chill has gone.
2 tsp sesame oil Cook the udon noodles according to packet instructions, reserving 1 cup (250ml) of noodle water.
400g udon noodles Heat a lightly greased barbecue or chargrill pan over high heat. Grill the steak for 3-4 minutes on each side until cooked to your liking.Remove from the heat and rest, loosely covered with foil, for 10-12 minutes.
50g unsalted butter, chopped Meanwhile, heat half the butter in a large non-stick frypan over medium-high heat. Add the mushroom and cook for 2-3 minutes until golden. Remove and set aside. Add the gochujang and tomato sauce to the pan and cook, stirring, for 1-2 minutes until the gochujang is lightly caramelised. Add the noodle water and bring to a simmer, cooking for 2-3 minutes until the liquid is slightly reduced.
150g mixed Asian mushrooms, sliced Add the noodles, spinach and remaining butter, and stir until spinach is wilted and noodles are coated in the sauce.
1/4 cup gochujang (Korean fermented chilli paste) Divide noodles among serving plates, top with sliced steak, broccolini, mushroom and nori. Sprinkle with sesame seeds to serve.
1 tbs tomato sauce
120g baby spinach leaves
1 bunch broccolini, cut in half on the diagonal, blanched
Chopped nori & toasted sesame seeds, to serve
