Lemon chicken

You will require: To prepare follow the instrution:
1 tsp cornflour Mix the cornflour with the soy sauce and lemon juice in a small bowl.
1 tsp dark soy sauce Heat the oil in a large frying pan or wok over a high heat, add the chicken, capsicum, carrot and broccoli and stir-fry for 2â€“3 minutes, or until the chicken is lightly browned and the vegetables are beginning to soften.
Finely grated zest & juice 1/2 small lemon Pour the lemon and soy mixture into the pan, add the chicken stock and spring onions and bring to a simmer. Reduce the heat and cook for 2 minutes, or until the sauce is slightly thickened and the chicken is cooked through, stirring regularly.
2 tsp coconut or canola oil Sprinkle with grated lemon zest and serve with rice.
1 skinless chicken breast fillet (around 150g), cut into 1.5cm slices
1 capsicum, any colour, deseeded and sliced
1 medium carrot (around 80g), trimmed and thinly sliced
100g broccoli, cut into small florets
150ml chicken stock (made with 1/2 Massel Plant Based Chicken Stock cube)
4 spring onions, trimmed and thickly sliced
